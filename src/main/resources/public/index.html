InnoQueue

Our app is about tracking household chores for roommates.

You create a queue for a certain chore and invite your friends. They complete the tasks one by one in a loop. So, ideally, every roommate should complete a task on each iteration.

The app reminds you about your to-do's and automates the whole process of keeping track of your chores.
